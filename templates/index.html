01 / the writer

I write because
I have to.

I'm Hariharan Vijayan — a 19-year-old from Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu. I write Tamil fiction. Not because it's a hobby. Because there are things that only make sense when they're written down.

I've just written one story.

I write about people who feel too much, love wrong, fail loudly, and still show up the next day. That's the only kind of story worth telling.
